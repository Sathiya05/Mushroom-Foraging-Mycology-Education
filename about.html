How It Started

From a local foray to a global network.

What began in 2018 as a small group of friends walking through the damp forests of the Pacific Northwest has blossomed into the world's premier mycology education hub.

We realized there was a dangerous gap between academic mycological journals and casual internet foraging advice. People wanted to learn, but they needed safe, science-backed, and accessible guidance.

Today, MycoSpore offers a complete pathway for fungi enthusiasts. Whether you want to safely identify a Choice Edible in the wild, or build a sterile flow-hood to clone your own genetics at home, we provide the tools, the community, and the education to make it happen.

Vision & Mission

Our Mission

To empower individuals with the knowledge to safely forage, sustainably harvest, and successfully cultivate fungi. We bridge the gap between academic mycology and everyday nature enthusiasts through accessible, science-backed education.

Our Vision

A world where humanity is reconnected to the forest floor. We envision a global network of ethical foragers and citizen scientists who protect mycelial networks and utilize fungi for medicine, food, and ecological restoration.

Environmental Commitment

Leave the Forest Better Than You Found It.

Mycology is deeply tied to the health of our ecosystems. We don't just teach you how to take from the forest; we teach you how to give back.

  • Mesh Bag Mandate

    All our recommended foraging gear uses breathable mesh to ensure spores drop back into the forest as you walk.

  • Sustainable Cultivation

    Our lab supplies prioritize biodegradable substrate bags and locally sourced, agricultural waste products.

Our Philosophy

The Tenets We Forage By

Accessible Science

We translate complex taxonomy and sterile lab procedures into easy-to-follow, practical curriculum for all skill levels.

Ethical Harvesting

We teach 'Leave No Trace' principles. Protecting the mycelial network and native forest habitats is our highest priority.

Open Community

Nature belongs to everyone. We foster an inclusive, supportive environment where experts share knowledge freely.
