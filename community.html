Rules of the Woods.

To maintain a safe and educational environment, all members must adhere to our core community guidelines. Violations regarding safety will result in immediate bans.

No 100% ID Guarantees

Online identification is never a substitute for in-person expert verification. Never eat a mushroom based solely on forum replies.

Protect Foraging Spots

Do not post exact GPS coordinates of wild edibles. Share general regions (e.g., "Pacific NW") to prevent over-harvesting by crowds.

Use Scientific Names

Common names vary by region and can be dangerous. Always strive to include the Latin binomial name (e.g., *Morchella elata*).

Require Good Photos

ID requests must include clear photos of the cap, gills/pores, stem, and base. Spore prints are highly encouraged.
