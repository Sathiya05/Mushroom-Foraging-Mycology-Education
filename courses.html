Still have questions?

Do I need a sterile lab to start?

No! Our beginner courses (like Kitchen Counter Cultivation) require zero lab equipment. We teach you how to build up to sterile work as you progress to advanced courses.

Are the classes live recorded?

All core curriculum videos are pre-recorded in stunning 4K, allowing you to learn at your own pace. However, we do host monthly live Q&A sessions for enrolled students.

Do I get a pro-level certificate?

Yes. Upon completing the final exam for any course, you will receive a digital MycoSpore Certification badge that gets added to your student profile and can be shared online.

Are spores/cultures included?

Physical supplies are not included in the digital course price. However, enrolled students receive a permanent 15% discount on all genetics and gear in our supply shop.
