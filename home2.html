Seasonal Foraging

What's Sprouting This Spring?

As the snow melts and the soil warms, the forest floor awakens. Spring is the premier season for some of the most sought-after gourmet mushrooms.

  • Black & Blonde Morels

    Look near dead elms, ash, and old apple orchards.

  • Dryad's Saddle (Pheasant Back)

    Smells like watermelon rind; found on dead hardwoods.

  • Oyster Mushrooms

    A cool-weather classic thriving on dying logs.
